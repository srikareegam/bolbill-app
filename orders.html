One-time setup
This feature reads handwriting using Google's free Gemini AI. It needs a free API key (takes ~2 minutes):
1. Open aistudio.google.com/apikey and sign in with your Google account
2. Tap Create API key
3. Copy the key and paste it below — it syncs to all your shop's devices
